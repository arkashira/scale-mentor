# Product Requirements Document
## Introduction
The scale-mentor project aims to provide a data-driven scaling solution for businesses, reducing trial-and-error time by ~30% with step-by-step playbooks and integrated KPI dashboards. This Product Requirements Document (PRD) outlines the product's vision, goals, key features, success metrics, and scope.

## Problem Statement
Businesses often struggle to scale efficiently, lacking access to actionable insights, automated workflows, and integrated tools. This leads to prolonged trial-and-error periods, resulting in wasted resources and lost opportunities.

## Target Users
The primary target users of scale-mentor are:

* Business owners and founders
* CEOs and CFOs
* Operations and finance teams
* Scaling and growth specialists

## Goals
The primary goals of scale-mentor are:

1. **Reduce trial-and-error time**: Provide data-driven scaling playbooks and automation to minimize the time spent on experimentation.
2. **Improve decision-making**: Offer integrated KPI dashboards and expert guidance to inform business decisions.
3. **Streamline operations**: Automate workflows and integrate with existing tools to reduce manual effort.

## Key Features
The following features are prioritized for the initial release:

1. **Scaling Playbooks**: Step-by-step guides for scaling businesses, built from case studies and industry expertise.
2. **KPI Dashboard**: Integrated dashboard for financial, operations, and hiring metrics, providing real-time insights and alerts.
3. **Automation**: Automated workflows for streamlined operations, including integration with Stripe, Xero, HubSpot, and more.
4. **On-demand Guidance**: Access to expert guidance and support, including personalized advice and best practices.
5. **Customization**: Tailored playbooks and automation to meet the unique needs of each business.

## Success Metrics
The success of scale-mentor will be measured by:

1. **User adoption**: The number of businesses using scale-mentor and the frequency of usage.
2. **Trial-and-error time reduction**: The percentage reduction in trial-and-error time for businesses using scale-mentor.
3. **Customer satisfaction**: User feedback and satisfaction ratings, measured through surveys and support requests.
4. **Revenue growth**: The increase in revenue for businesses using scale-mentor, compared to those not using the platform.

## Scope
The initial release of scale-mentor will focus on the following:

* Development of scaling playbooks and KPI dashboard
* Integration with Stripe, Xero, HubSpot, and other key tools
* Automation of workflows and expert guidance
* Customization and onboarding process for businesses

The following features are out of scope for the initial release:

* Advanced AI-powered forecasting and prediction
* Integration with niche or industry-specific tools
* Custom development for individual businesses

## Technical Requirements
The scale-mentor platform will be built using Python, with a focus on scalability, security, and maintainability. The tech stack will include:

* Python 3.9+
* pyproject.toml and requirements.txt for dependency management
* A modular architecture, with separate components for playbooks, KPI dashboard, automation, and integration

## Project Timeline
The project timeline will be divided into the following phases:

1. **Research and planning**: 2 weeks
2. **Development**: 12 weeks
3. **Testing and QA**: 4 weeks
4. **Launch and deployment**: 2 weeks

## Conclusion
The scale-mentor project aims to provide a data-driven scaling solution for businesses, reducing trial-and-error time and improving decision-making. By focusing on key features, success metrics, and technical requirements, we can deliver a high-quality platform that meets the needs of our target users.
